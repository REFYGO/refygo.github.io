Woo(Bipedal robot)@B3

This was a 20 dof bipedal robot. The robot consists of esp32, serial servos, and foot sensors. The foot sensor measured ZMP and was used for gait stabilization. The robot could also funny things like handstand, but there are no other videos. I am thinking of restomoding this robot but have not done anything...

Specification

Name Model Quantities Description
Servo motor VS-SV410 20 41kgf-cm@16.8V
Foot sensor(FSR) - 8 For measuring ZMP
Micro controller ESP-WROOM-32 1 Controlled by smartphone via bluetooth
